Episto

Record. Summarize. Understand.

Mission

Description

Episto intends to change the way users interact with audio recordings. A significant amount of time is wasted when just mindlessly playing a recording of an important meeting, class, or other event back to yourself. Using Episto, only the important parts of the recording are played back which would save time for the user.

Benefit

Episto is, in many ways, the natural next step for virtual assistants. This application of technology could allow virtual assistants like Siri to act on their own without inalienating the user.

The Episto method of recording comprehension saves tremendous amounts of time by only listening to things that are important. In most cases, meetings are filled with a lot of unnecessary discussion and a lot less important points. With Episto, listen to only what is important and save tremendous amounts of time.

Architecture

The User Perspective

The user will:

  1. Open up the Episto App
  2. Record a meeting from his/her browser or phone
  3. End the recording and hit the process button
  4. Receive a summary of the meeting by email and browser
  5. Smile

Data flows from the client to the Google Speech API, then the text summarizer and information extractor, outputting useful information.

"Important Information"

  • Meeting Summary (Most Important Statements)
  • Names and other Contact Information
  • Times and Places
  • Action Items
